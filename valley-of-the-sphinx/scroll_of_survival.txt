	    Scroll of Survival

Read through this scroll carefully.

In order for you to coax the sphinx into giving you hints on the riddles he ask, offer
him treasures that you have collected from other pyramids.
Magical items can also be used to either defeat a sphinx in battle or compell him to help you.

Always remember to best the sphinx 
one must think logically, but not literally.
The secret to discerning the correct 
answer lies within the word play of
the question.
